Frequently Asked Questions

How are you able to tutor virtually?

We use high-quality technology as a tool to help us conduct our virtual tutoring sessions. We are well-versed and comfortable with modern tech tools, and through computers and tablets we are able to view your child's projects and assignments, mark up PDFs and documents, while sharing and streaming it all in real-time right in front of your student through their own computer, tablet, or phone.

What are the advantages of virtual tutoring?

At NextLevel, our virtual tutoring allows for exceptional flexibility, both for students as well as our tutors. As long as your student has access to an internet connection, we will be able to conduct a tutoring session even from across the country.

Virtual tutoring also allows many of the same benefits as in-person tutoring, such as an individualized teaching style catered to your student, a positive and encouraging study space to learn and grow, and improved academic performance through guided and focused efforts on problem areas, all with the added safety of a virtual-first approach.

What are the disadvantages of virtual tutoring?

There is no replacement for the classroom. However, in difficult and uncertain times like today, virtual tutoring serves as a vital supplement to a student's education.

It's reasonable to expect a few technical hiccups at the beginning with virtual tutoring as we figure out how to best make our technology work together, but the process and communication will be improved over time.

What is the cost of your tutoring services?

Our services start at $50 per session for our virtual tutoring services, which last around 55 minutes long. We offer several packages to add tutoring credits to your account, allowing you to be flexible in scheduling sessions that fit your schedule.

Do you offer group tutoring?

Yes, we offer group tutoring but with a couple of caveats. Due to the unpredictability and variability of bandwidth and ISP providers, we will only take a maximum of 4 students per session. For groups of 2, we charge $35 for each student, and for groups of 3 and 4 we charge $25 for each student.

Do you offer your services on a sliding scale?

Please contact us if you are needing private tutoring services and are experiencing financial difficulties. We will do our best to reach an agreement that works for all parties.

Do you teach curriculum?

No. Our tutoring is designed to supplement your child’s school education and is not meant to be a replacement for compulsory education. If we are providing a tutoring service that is not categorized as Academic Subject tutoring, we utilize an experiential and project-based approach to learning.

Why is it nextleveltutoring.io? Where does .io come from?

.io is a top level domain that’s typically used for organizations that want to be associated with technology. This is because in computer science, I/O stands for input/output. As a virtual tutoring service that utilizes modern technology to help our students, we felt that taking a .io domain would be appropriate.
